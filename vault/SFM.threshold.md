#threshold

Follow up to Liu and Chen 2016

Liu and Chen 2020 JoE

Liu and Chen extend static factor models and allow the loading matrix (which they denote confusingly as $A$) to be different according to a thresholding mechanism.

## Implementation

Liu and Chen did have R code, though this was awful to follow up and basically only worked for their simulation study.

An attempt was made at simplifying this and making it work for normal data.

Preliminary results showed that this algorithm had significant convergence issues, and I'm not sure why.
