## Parametric Solutions

Some preprint papers have suggested to parametrically model COVID as nothing more than an explosion in variance, that then decays periodically.

I don't like this approach, because this basically treats COVID as an entirely exogenenous shock to variance, and only has an effect on the variance of different data series. This is in contrast to what economists tend to think.
