## Summary

In summary, regardless of which set of technical assumptions, we require:

- The factors to be pervasive (have non neglible effects on *most* of the variables)
- the idiosyncratic components to be weakly cross-correlated 

which will allow us to disentangle the common component from the idiosyncratic component by means of aggregation, as $N \rightarrow \infty$.
