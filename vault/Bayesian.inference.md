## Bai and Wang (2012)

A fairly comprehensive looking paper that derives important results for estimating truly dynamic factor models via the Bayesian Paradigm.

The main draw of this approach is that it requires much fewer restrictions to achieve identification of the factors. Thus, the usual set of restrictions can then become overidentification restrictions, and presumably result in efficiency gains.
