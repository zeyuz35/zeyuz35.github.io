## Overall Requirements

3 papers, ideally published by the end of the PhD.

## Confirmation (1st year)

[x] Get 70 in BEX6500

[x] Get 70 in BEX6510

### Oral Presentation

- 20 minutes presentation in open forum
- Research undertaken to date 

### Written Submission

1. Title of Thesis
2. Background and Motivation for the research questions being addressed in the thesis
3. Overview of thesis

    - Brief detail of proposed content of all chapters
    - Description of theoretical and conceptual framework that underlies thesis
    - procedures that are to be used in addressing the research questions
  
4. Timetable for completing thesis and statement of progress to date
5. Bibliography of reference that appear in the report

This report must be submitted 1 week prior to the actual panel date

## Progress Review (2nd Year)

### Oral Presentation

- 30 minutes presentation
- Explores research undertaken to date
- Anticipated future direction of program
- Describe in detail, at least one element of the research program
- Content must be distinct from confirmation

### Written Submission

- Length is flexible
1. Title
2. Brief overview of findings to data
3. Proposed thesis structure
4. More detailed explanation of an element (e.g. chapter) that was presented orally
5. Timetable for completing the thesis + detailed statement of progress towards this work plan
6. Identification of any potential difficulties that could impede progress

Any papers that are working/submitted/accepted are also to be submitted

If this paper is related to the element that was presented, requirement 4 is not needed

## Final Review (3rd year, pre-submission!)

### Oral Presentation

- Full Length public seminar
1. Summary of thesis
2. Describes in detail, at least one element of the program

### Written Submission

Length is flexible

1. Title of Thesis
2. Overview of Thesis
3. Proposed Thesis structure
4. More detailed explanation of the element presented orally
5. Timetable for completing the thesis
6. Identification of any perceived difficulties that could impede completion of project according to work plan
