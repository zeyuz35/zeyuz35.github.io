Factor Models in Finance

Factor models in finance are aimed at analysing either stock returns, or their volatility (conditional variance).

  1. Arbitrage Pricing Theory and optimal vestment portfolios
  2. Network analysis in idosyncratic components
  3. Parametric Models for large conditional variance matrices

APT

Stock returns are typically observed to have low autocorrelation and therefore are modelled as whtie noises. In this case, a static factor model seems appropriate.
